About me

Hi, Im Enalin Alabastro. 22 years old living in gun-ob lapu-lapu. I believe in the saying “The most important thing is to enjoy your life—to be happy—it's all that matters.”

Traveler

As a traveler, I am fueled by curiosity and a thirst for adventure. I immerse myself in diverse cultures, savoring every moment and forging connections with people along the way. I believe travel is about more than just seeing new places; it's about understanding and embracing the world's rich tapestry of experiences, and I cherish every opportunity to explore, learn, and grow


Nature Lover

As a nature lover, I find solace and inspiration in the beauty of the natural world. From towering mountains to serene forests and tranquil rivers, I am drawn to the wonders of the outdoors. Exploring pristine landscapes fills me with a sense of awe and reverence, and I am committed to preserving and protecting our planet for future generations. Whether I'm hiking through rugged terrain, camping beneath the stars, or simply taking a moment to appreciate a colorful sunset, my connection to nature enriches my life and fuels my passion for exploration.
